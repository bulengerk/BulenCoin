# BulenCoin – 10‑second ad scenarios (English, no UI shots)

Each spot is voiceover‑driven, simple visuals (devices, icons, maps), and ends with the same CTA: “Run a node on your phone or laptop. BulenCoin.io”.

1) **Runs everywhere**  
   - Shots: phone on a café table, laptop in a dorm, tiny server LED.  
   - VO: “BulenCoin runs on phones, laptops and servers. One network, same rewards for staying online.”  
   - End slate CTA.

2) **No mining rigs**  
   - Shots: crossed‑out mining farm → quiet desk with a charging phone.  
   - VO: “No noisy rigs. Stake, stay online, earn automatically.”  
   - CTA.

3) **Uptime pays**  
   - Shots: ticking timer, green checkmarks.  
   - VO: “Keep your BulenCoin node alive, get paid. Uptime is the new hashpower.”  
   - CTA.

4) **Choose your role**  
   - Shots: icons for phone (mobile light), laptop (full), cloud (gateway).  
   - VO: “Pick a profile that fits your device. You’re part of the chain in seconds.”  
   - CTA.

5) **Payments ready**  
   - Shots: QR code overlaid on a payment confirmation icon.  
   - VO: “On‑chain payments with simple APIs and wallet‑friendly links. BulenCoin moves in real time.”  
   - CTA.

6) **Security by default**  
   - Shots: lock icons for signatures, P2P token, TLS badge.  
   - VO: “Signatures required. P2P token. TLS optional. BulenCoin ships with safe defaults.”  
   - CTA.

7) **Autonomous rewards**  
   - Shots: coin stream splitting into burn, ecosystem, producer, stakers.  
   - VO: “BulenCoin splits block rewards and fees every block. No admin buttons, just protocol.”  
   - CTA.

8) **Mobile friendly**  
   - Shots: battery icon stays green, data saver symbol.  
   - VO: “Mobile‑light respects battery and data. Run a BulenCoin node without draining your phone.”  
   - CTA.

9) **Community validators**  
   - Shots: world map lighting up with city pins.  
   - VO: “Community validators everywhere. Small or big stake, uptime matters.”  
   - CTA.

10) **Open source**  
   - Shots: code scrolling, green “tests passed” stamp.  
   - VO: “Open‑source BulenCoin client, tests included. Run it on your phone, laptop or server.”  
   - CTA “BulenCoin.io”.
