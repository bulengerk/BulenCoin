# Brand Usage – Do / Don’t

## Do

- Use SVG logo on dark backgrounds (#0b1024 / #0f1633) with sufficient contrast.
- Keep clearspace: at least 0.5× logo height around the mark/wordmark.
- Use accent gradients (turquoise → magenta → amber) sparingly for CTAs/highlights.
- Pair the logo only with BulenCoin palette and approved typography (Space Grotesk + Manrope).
- Provide alt text: “BulenCoin logo”.

## Don’t

- Don’t stretch, skew or recolor the logo outside the palette.
- Don’t place the mark on busy/low-contrast photos; use an overlay or solid background.
- Don’t apply drop shadows, bevels or outlines to the logo.
- Don’t combine the logo with other brands’ marks without spacing and permission.
- Don’t change the letter spacing or retype the wordmark.
